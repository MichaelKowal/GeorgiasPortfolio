About

  • 1994 - 2016

    Growing and Learning

    I grew up in Prince Rupert, BC. Prince Rupert is a small town on the North Coast known for its fishing and its rain. I've always loved creating things and when I discovered the world of programming I dove in head first. For years I did different tutorials on different languages and software tools, until I finally decided to get a computer science degree.

  • September 2016

    Fresh Start

    Going to university was the first time I had ever left Prince Rupert. It provided many new challenges and opportunities. I've since made many new friends, had new experiences, and learned so much. The classroom environment really boosted my learning in ways I did not expect. There is a lot more to the field of computer science than you can learn from a few youtube videos!

  • Summer 2018

    Work!

    During the summer of 2018 I was hired by one of my professors to build Pulpatect. I worked 8-4 every day applying the skills I had learned in school as well as learning how to work on a larger team. It was a fantastic experience that proved to me that I had chosen the right field.

  • April 2019

    Graduation!

    I will be graduating with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science in April of 2019. As soon as my exams are done I will be moving to Victoria to look for work in the tech industry and start the next chapter of my life.
